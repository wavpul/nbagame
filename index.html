How To Play
1

Pull the lever to spin all three wheels: a Base Player, a Modifier Player, and an Attribute.

2

Your card keeps the Base Player's body and stats, except the rolled Attribute — that gets replaced by the Modifier Player's rating.

3

Each wheel gets one respin for the whole game, guaranteed to land on something new.

4

Click an open roster slot to lock your card in. Slots are permanent — fill all 6 to unlock the season simulation.
